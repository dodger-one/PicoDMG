## Context

Trying to reach full-speed emulation (~60 FPS) on RP2040.

Current ceiling: ~54 FPS.

## What I tried

- Increased CPU clock (266 → 300 MHz)
- SPI frequency tuning
- Interlaced rendering

## Results

- Clock increase → no real gain
- SPI → not the main bottleneck
- Interlace → reaches 60 FPS but introduces artifacts

## Conclusion

The bottleneck is likely in rendering + timing sync, not raw CPU.

## Next steps

- Move audio to core1
- Optimize framebuffer writes
